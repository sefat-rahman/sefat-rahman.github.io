GASP: Gradient-Aware Shortest Paths For Boundary Confined 2-Manifold Reeb Graph Visualization

Reeb Graph Visualization ‚Ä¢ 2025

We present GASP, an algorithm for visualizing Reeb graphs on 2-manifolds that constrains arcs to the surface, minimizes their length, and aligns them with the function gradient. Compared to the geometric barycenter approach, GASP yields more faithful and compact representations of topological structures, with demonstrated improvements through qualitative and quantitative evaluation

Uncertainty Visualization ‚Ä¢ 2025

This project focuses on the interactive 3D rendering of Reeb graphs and persistent homology diagrams with real-time parameter adjustment.
